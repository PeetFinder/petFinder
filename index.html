About PetFinder

Our Mission, Our Vision, and the Community Behind the System

Our Mission

The primary goal of PetFinder is to serve as a bridge for the fast and safe return of lost pets to their loving families. Through innovative web technology and active community participation, we streamline incident reporting and tracking using a real-time locator system.

Our Vision

We envision a compassionate and interconnected community where no pet is ever left missing or abandoned on the streets. PetFinder aims to become the leading web utility platform in the country for animal welfare, community safety, and responsive locator assistance.

To report a missing pet, you need to create an account or log in first. Once logged in, click on the "Report Lost Pet" button on the dashboard. Fill out the application form with your pet's name, species, breed, distinct markings, a recent photograph, and the last known location where they went missing.

If you happen to see an animal that matches a missing report, go to that pet's specific profile panel and click the "Sighting" button. You can submit a short tip or text log detailing the exact time and precise landmark coordinates where you spotted the animal to alert the owner immediately.

Privacy Policy

How We Protect and Handle Your Personal Information

1. Information We Collect

When you use PetFinder Hub, we collect standard credentials necessary for core feature tracking. This includes your complete name, authenticated email address, custom login credentials, and public contact information necessary to establish owner communication linkages.

2. Use of System Content & Pet Data

All data related to missing animals—including pet names, descriptions, species identifiers, uploaded images, and last known geographic parameters—is deliberately published out to the open dashboard feed to alert community observers. Landmark logs submitted via the locator panel serve solely to assist active retrieval procedures.

3. Data Exposure & Map Geocoding

To fulfill locator assistance parameters, specific sighting inputs and relative landmark coordinates are converted into visual map marks on our public geocoding canvas. We advise against supplying precise private residential data unless strictly crucial for pet identification and return protocols.

4. Security Measures & Retention

We employ strict structural design approaches to store profile information securely and safeguard it from unapproved external intercepts. Data records remain cached on our server blocks within the duration of active status operations, and users retain full rights to wipe or alter logs directly through their active profile channels.

5. Policy Changes

The system administration reserves the capacity to amend privacy conditions to match security engineering standards or adjust configuration needs. Continued data processing and login activity within the layout after revisions take place imply consent to structural policy adaptations.
